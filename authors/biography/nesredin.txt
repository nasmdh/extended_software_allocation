Nesredin received his B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at Mekelle Institute of Technology, Ethiopia in 2007, and M.Sc. in Intelligent Embedded Systems at M{\"a}lardalen University, Sweden, in 2013. Since 2014, Nesredin is a Ph.D. student at M{\"a}lardalen University,  and is a member of the Network and Embedded System (NES) division and the Formal Modeling and Analysis research group. His research focus are application of formal methods in safety-critical embedded systems, design space exploration in distributed computing, and schedulability analysis of real-time systems.
