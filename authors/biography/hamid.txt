Hamid Reza Faragardi received a B.Sc. in computer engineering in 2010 and a M.Sc. in software engineering in 2012 from the University of Tehran. He was a PhD candidate in Malardalen University, Sweden in computer engineering from 2013 to 2017. Since January 2018 to March 2019 he was a postdoc researcher at the University of Innsbruck. He is now a lecturer at the KTH Royal Institute of technology in Stockholm where he is supervising multiple Master students in the context of Artificial Intelligence, at the same time, he works as a researcher at the Ericsson Research Center in Stockholm, Sweden. His main research interests include Resource Allocation, Optimization Problems, Edge Computing, and IoT.
